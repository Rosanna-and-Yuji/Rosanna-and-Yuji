What is the dresscode?

Floral, Flowy, or Funky

Pick your vibe or mix them all—

🌸 Floral – Embrace nature with flowers or botanical prints.(Think: a floral dress, a Hawaiian shirt, or a leafy-patterned jumpsuit.)
🌬️ Flowy – Breezy fabrics and relaxed silhouettes.(Think: a maxi dress, wide-leg pants, or a billowy blouse.)
🎭 Funky – Bold colors, quirky accessories, or something unexpected!(Think: statement socks, patterned suits, or a hot pink dress.)

No need to buy anything new—just get creative with what’s in your closet. We can’t wait to celebrate with you in style!
